Duke Nukem's Lady Killer Casino.


On May 9th 2022, a group of intrepid anonymous users going by the name "x0r_jmp" uploaded one of the most sought-after unreleased games of all time: the 2001 iteration of Duke Nukem Forever. Bearing little to no resemblance to the final 2011 version of the game, the 2001 builds of the game, in retrospect, show the incredible dedication by the team at 3DRealms and the amazing amount of promise the game held at that time.


This resulted in a flurry of renewed interest in the franchise, attracting veteran Duke Nukem fans and new eyes alike. That's where we came in in. Just days after the builds were leaked online, the nucleus of our team was formed. The team quickly blossomed into over 100 volunteers, working remotely at all hours of the day to restore broken features, polish existing levels, and rebuild the game's engine for modern systems.
